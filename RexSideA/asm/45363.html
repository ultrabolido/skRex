B133: Room directions and enemy pointers
Every room spans 6 bytes. The first four bytes indicates the connections of the room (top, left, bottom and right). A value of $00 indicates no room connection
Byte Content
$00 Top room
$01 Right room
$02 Bottom room
$03 Left room
$04 - $05 Address pointer for enemy configuration on this room
